Identifying Errors in Jupyter Notebooks

Do you use Jupyter Notebooks and/or Python for your coding/scripting activities?

If so, I'd like to learn more about how you work with the tool and identify errors within data analyses.


In this study, we ask you to explore an existing notebook that contains errors.

The study takes place over Zoom and you may participate from anywhere you feel comfortable.

We estimate participating will take about 60-90 minutes of your time.

We hope that our observations can help guide suggestions on how to more effectively teach error identification methods.


Note we are not evaluating your skills, but rather we want to learn what approaches you and other participants use for identifying data analysis errors.


If you are interested in participating you may signup here.


If you're unsure if you fit the requirements please feel free to email me.


You may verify the ethical approval of this study or raise any concerns you might have by contacting me directly or the Human Research Ethics Office at the University of Victoria (250-472-4545 or ethics@uvic.ca).
