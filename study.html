Identifying Errors in Jupyter Notebooks

Do you use Jupyter Notebooks, Python, and Pandas for your data analysis/exploration activities?
Would you like to earn a $25 Amazon Gift Card?


If so, I'd like to interview you over Zoom for roughly one hour while you identify errors in a pre-written Jupyter Notebook.
This interview is not an evaluation of your skills, rather a time for me to learn how you approach the task of identifying errors.
I hope that what I learn from you can help guide suggestions on how to more effectively teach error identification methods.

If you are interested in participating you may signup here.

If you're unsure if you fit the requirements please email me.

You may verify the ethical approval of this study or raise any concerns you might have by contacting myself directly or the Human Research Ethics Office at the University of Victoria (250-472-4545 or ethics@uvic.ca).
